ToothGrowth Data Description

The response is the length of odontoblasts (teeth) in each of 10 guinea pigs at each of three dose levels of Vitamin C (0.5, 1, and 2 mg) with each of two delivery methods (orange juice or ascorbic acid).

We can plot the seperate effect of dose on length, and the seperate effect supplement on length.

suppressMessages(library(gridExtra))

grid.arrange(plot2.1, plot2.2, ncol = 2)

As this plot shows the seperate effect of dose and supplement on length, it will help us in making hypothesis for step 3.
